Sugar Cookies

Description

This absolutely delectable recipe for sugar cookies will take you through a step-by-step process to creating the perfect treat for literally any occasion!

These cookies can be made in absolutely any shape or size, ready to be decorated exactly as you desire! A fun family night of baking or the best pick-me-up snack awaits you!

Ingredients

Yields 60 servings
  • 1.5 cups butter
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 TSP real vanilla extract
  • 5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 TSP baking powder
  • 1 TSP salt

Steps

  1. Use an electric mixer to combine softened butter and sugar until smoothened. Then beat in your vanilla and eggs. Next, stir in flour, baking powder, and salt. Cover the mixture, and allow to chill overnight (or 1 hour minimum)
  2. Preheat your oven to 400 degrees Farenheit or 200 degrees Celcius
  3. Dust your counter with flour. Roll your dough to a thickness between 1/4 and 1/2 of an inch.
  4. Cut your sugar cookies into the desired shape.
  5. Place cookies 1 inch apart on an ungreased baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 6-8 minutes or until cookies are lightly browned.
  7. Transfer cookies to a wire rack until they are fully cooled down and dig in!
