Fresh Guacamole

Description

This mouth watering guacamole recipe is quick and easy to make. It will absolutely add that extra touch of flavour needed in your dish as the fresh and savoury notes delight your mouth.

Ingredients

Yields 4 servings
  • 2 avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, minced. (Seeded for a more mild guac)
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
Steps
  1. Peel and pit your avocados
  2. Mash them inside of a bowl until they are broken down and creamy
  3. Mix in the tomatoes, onion, cilantro, lemon juice, and jalapenos until they are combined
  4. Add salt and black pepper as needed and dig in!
