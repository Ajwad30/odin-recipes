Perfect homemade lasagna

Ingredients (Yields 12 servings)
  • 1lb sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4lb lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 crushed garlic gloves
  • 28oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 6oz cans of tomato paste
  • 2 6.5oz cans of tomato sauce
  • 0.5 cup water
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 1.5 tsp dried basil leaves
  • 0.5 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 0.25 tsp ground black pepper
  • 4 tbsp fresh chopped parsley
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 0.75lbs sliced mozzarella cheese
  • 0.75 cups grated parmesan cheese

This amazingly rich and hearty lasagna recipe is a guaranteed showstopper! Serve on a large weekend gathering filled with family and friends, all you have to do after that is sit back and watch as your loved ones confuse you for Gordon Ramsay.

Here are the steps to making this dish:

  1. Set the heat to medium and cook the sausage, ground beef, onions, and garlic until they are browned.
  2. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato pasta, tomato sauce, and water. Season this with sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, 1tsp of salt, pepper, and 2tbsp of parsley.
  3. Cover and allow to simmer for roughly an hour and a half, occasionally stirring.
  4. Fill a large pot with water and lightly salt before bringing to a boil. Add your lasagna noodles and cook for 8-10 minutes before draining the noodles and rinsing with cold water.
  5. Combine ricotta cheese, egg, leftover parsley, and 1/2 tsp salt in a mixing bowl.
  6. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees F or 190 degrees C.
  7. Spread 1.5 cups of meat sauce at the bottom of a 9x13 baking dish. Place 6 noodles lengthwise over meatsauce and spread half of the ricotta cheese mix overtop of the noodles. Place 1/3'rd of the mozzarella cheese slices on top. Spread another 1.5 cups of meat sauce on top of the mozarella along with 1/4 cup of parmesan cheese.
  8. Repeat these layering steps and spread remaining mozzarella and parmesean at the top.
  9. Cover your baking dish with foil, making sure the cheese does not touch the foil (Alternatively, spray the foil with cooking spray) in order to prevent sticking.
  10. Bake in oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 25 minutes.
  11. Allow to cool for 15 minutes before cutting in and enjoying.
